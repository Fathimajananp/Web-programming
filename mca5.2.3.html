MCA

The Department of Master of Computer Applications (MCA) was started under KMCT School of Business (KSB) was in the year 2007 with a total in take capacity of 60. Currently, the department has a sanctioned intake of 30. The department always aims to produce versatile IT professionals capable to adopt changing needs in computer applications and research activities. The PG programme offered by the department follows the curriculum and syllabi as well as rules and regulations of APJ ABDUL KALAM Technological University.

The department envisions to focus on academic excellence and to train versatile IT professionals capable of adopting to the changing needs in the field of computer applications and research

Provide knowledge of emerging trends in Computer Science and to equip the students to find workable solutions to the complex industrial problems with awareness and concern for society and environment.
